Patrol Instructions

Keep your eyes on the water line when you are on patrol, and you will see stranded sea turtles in the surf as the tide is cresting. Do the following when you spot a beached turtle:

arrow_upward

Move turtle above tide line

ac_unit

Cover turtle with seaweed to shelter from wind

place

Mark spot with beach debris

Report your location here or click here for reporting instructions
